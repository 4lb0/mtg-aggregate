What is this?

This is a way to generate an average deck list from an archetype by using the algorithm described by Frank Karsten in his article A New Way to Determine an Aggregate Deck List.

Now also supports Pokémon TCG deck lists! Upload multiple Pokémon deck files to get an aggregated deck with separate sections for Pokémon, Trainer, and Energy cards.
